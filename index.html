About me

I like to imagine, and I like to create. This can sometimes lead to a reaction as spontaneous as combustion with innovation as the resulting "byproduct." This hacker culture was ingrained in me during my years at MIT. Whether I am building with my 3D printer or going on new adventures, I keep myself busy but always in the moment. I stay sane by regularly working out at the gym, running 5K's along the Charles River, and keeping up with Woj on the latest in the NBA.

My experiences

Massachusetts Institute of Technology

I was a Course 6 (Electrical Engineering and Computer Science) student who completed the Pre-Med track with a Minor in 7 (Biology) and a humanities concentration in Political Science. Class of 2018.

United States Archery Team

I first picked up a bow and arrows back in 2010. Over the years, I was fortunate to train under USA National Head Coach Kisik Lee, and I am grateful for the selections to the 2014 Junior US Archery Team and USA Archery Junior Dream Team.

Healthcare Sales Engineer

I have been working as a Healthcare Sales Engineer at InterSystems in Cambridge, MA since July 2018. I regularly travel all over the US to promote interoperability and innovation in the healthcare IT sector.
