Recent Works

Alexa Custom Skill Development

I used the Alexa Skills Kit SDK for Node.js to develop a custom Alexa skill called "Restaurant Finder". I wanted to be able to quickly find a few well-liked restaurants based on my preferred cuisine without having to take out my phone and scour through reviews to find a good place to eat. This Alexa skill asks users what they want to eat and in what city they are looking for restaurants. Based on those inputs, I leveraged Yelp Fusion's REST API to tell users the top 3 most-reviewed restaurants of their craving that are open and within a 3 mile radius. Check it out on the Alexa Skills Store or take a look at the code on my Github.


Imaging Software Development for Aortic Stenosis Diagnosis

I was an undergraduate researcher at the Edelman Lab (Harvard-MIT Biomedical Engineering Center) from January 2017 to May 2018. One of my main projects was to improve the ML-based algorithm developed by previous researchers to quantify calcium deposits on the aortic valve using CT images of the heart. Previous algorithm was capable of analyzing individual 2D slices from CT scans. I incorporated 3D image processing capabilities for a more accurate analysis, in addition to adding an option for users to verify the quantification with 3D models, to improve the reliability and accuracy of the computed quantification metrics.
